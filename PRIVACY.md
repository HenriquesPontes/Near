# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 6, 2026

## 1. Information We Collect
Near does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). All Bluetooth scanning and device detection happens entirely locally on your device.

## 2. Bluetooth and Location Data
- **Bluetooth:** The app uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to detect nearby smart glasses and camera-equipped wearables. The app only reads publicly broadcasted advertising packets.
- **Location:** iOS requires Location Services permission to scan for Bluetooth devices in the background. We do not track, record, or share your GPS location.

## 3. Data Storage
Any saved devices (e.g., "Starred" devices) are stored locally on your device using SwiftData. We do not have access to this data, and it is never uploaded to any server.

## 4. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer, Henriques Pontes.
